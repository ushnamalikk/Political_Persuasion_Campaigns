Aur jab woh aasani se seekhein ge, to un ki takhleeqi salahiyaat mein izafa ho ga aur woh zyada khud aitemaadi ke saath taraqqi karein ge. Kya aap nahi chahte ke hamare bachay fakhar mehsoos karein ke woh apni saqafat ko bacha rahe hain?

Ab zara is par bhi sochiye ke jab woh bare ho kar mukhtalif shobon mein kaam karein ge, to un ka mazboot buniyad hona zaroori hai. Hamari maadri zubaan humein duniya ke kisi bhi kone mein apni shanakht barqarar rakhne mein madad deti hai. Agar hum apni zubaan ko pas-pusht dal dein ge, to hamari shanakht kahan jaaye gi?

Boht se mulkon ne apni zubaan ko ahmiyat di hai aur isi buniyad par taraqqi ki hai. Japan, Cheen, Germany—har ek ne apne bachon ko apni zubaan mein taleem di aur aaj woh sab taraqqi yafta mulk hain. Humein bhi apne bachon ko wahi moqa dena chahiye jo in mulkon ne diye hain.

Aaiye ek lamha ke liye tasawar karein ke hamare bachay urdu mein taleem haasil kar rahe hain. Un ke dilon mein hamare Sha'iron ke naghme, hamari tareekh ki kahaniyan, aur hamari saqafat ki bharpoor jhalkiyan bas rahi hain. Kya woh bachay chamakte sitarhe nahi baneinge jo apni roshni se duniya ko roshan karein ge?

Yeh faisla bohat aasan hai. Humein apne bachon ki behtareen taleem aur un ki khudi ko mehfooz rakhne ke liye urdu ko tarjeeh deni chahiye. Aaiye, ek saath mil kar, hamare bachon aur mulk ke mustaqbil ko mazboot banayein. Shukriya.


3b010
Pyari behan bhaiyo, aik baat par ghor kijiye: kya hum apni zaban aur shanakht ko bhool rahe hain? Angrezi zaban ko hum itni ahmiyat kyun de rahe hain? Zara sochiye, sab se pehle to, Quaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah ne khud kaha tha ke qoamein apni zaban se taraqqi karti hain. Urdu hamari qaumi zaban hai, hamari pehchaan hai.

Ab aik hairan kun haqeeqat suno. Hali mein hui tehqeeq se pata chala hai ke woh bachay jo apni madri zaban mein tales hasil karte hain, unki qabiliyat aur samajhne ki salahiyat kahin zyada hoti hai. UNESCO jaise muatabar idaray bhi mante hain ke bachay apni zaban mein behtar seekhte hain.

Zara sochiye, agar hamare bachay Urdu mein taleem hasil karein, to woh apni tareekh, saqafat aur aqdar ko behtar samajh sakenge. Angrezi Bar-e-Sagheer mein angrezon ki chhodi hui nishani hai jo humein apni zaban se door kar rahi hai.

Aur aik aur baat, kya aap jaante hain ke German, Japani aur Cheeni qaumein apni zaban mein taleem hasil karke duniya ki maqbool tareen qaumon mein shamil hui hain? Yeh qaumein apni zaban par fakhar karti hain aur humein bhi fakhar karna chahiye.

Jab aap apne bachon ko Urdu mein taleem dilayenge, to aap unki jaden mazboot karenge. Woh apni shanakht par fakhar karenge aur aik sache Pakistani banenge. Aaiye, aik azm karein ke Urdu ko hi apni taleemi zaban banayenge. Yeh hamari shanakht hai, yeh hamari zaban hai, yeh hamari taqat hai.

To aayein, mil kar faisla karein ke hamare bachay Urdu mein hi taleem hasil karein aur apni zaban ko zinda rakhein. Yaqeen maniay, yahi rasta hamari taraqqi ka zamin hai.


3b011
Bhai jan, aap ne kabhi socha ke hamari apni zubaan, Urdu, kitni khoobsurat aur dil ko choo lene wali hai? Jaante hain ke hamare bachay jab Urdu mein parhai karte hain, to wo apne culture ke qareeb rehte hain. Unhein apni saqafat aur zubaan par fakhar hai. To phir hum kyun piche rahein?

Aur ye bhi to hai ke jab bacha Urdu mein parhne likhne lagega, to wo apni zubaan mein ziyada khud aitemadi mehsoos karega. Khud aitemadi zindagi mein kamyabi ke liye kitni zaroori hai, ye to aap bhi jaante hain.

Hamari apni zubaan, apni pehchaan hai. Hum apni zubaan ko tark kar ke, apni shanakht kyun khoein? Angrezi seekhni to zaroori hai, magar buniyadi taleem apni zubaan mein ho to faida ziyada hota hai.

Bhai, dil par haath rakh kar sochain. Kya aap waqai chahte hain ke hamari aanay wali naslein apni jaroon se door ho jayein? Urdu ko taleem ka buniyadi zariya bana kar hum na sirf apni saqafat ko bacha sakte hain, balkay bachon ki behtar tarbiyat bhi kar sakte hain. Ab faisla aap ke haath mein hai.


3b100
Dekhein, har aik walidain aur ustad chahta hai ke unke bachay behtareen taleem hasil karein, aur yeh bilkul durust hai. Humein sab se pehle yeh samajhna hoga ke zuban taleem mein aik bohat ahem kirdar ada karti hai. Hum sab is baat par muttahid hain ke hamare bachon ko behtar mustaqbil ke liye mazboot buniyadon ki zaroorat hai.

Ab aayein dekhte hain ke Urdu kyun behtareen zariya taleem ho sakti hai. Urdu hamari qaumi zuban hai, jise hamare bachay ghar se hi jaante hain. Professor Alex Johnson jo Harvard University mein taleem ke shobe se taluq rakhte hain, unki tehqiqat mein yeh zahir hua ke bachon ko ibtida mein madri zuban mein parhana unhein zyada aitimaad deta hai aur unki takhleqi salahiyat ko mazeed nikhar deta hai.

Lihaza, humein is baat par ghour karna chahiye ke bachon ki behtareen taraqqi aur unke mustaqbil ke liye Urdu hi behtareen zariya taleem hai. Kudrati zuban mein taleem se bachay na sirf kamyab hote hain balke apni shanakht ko bhi mehfooz rakhte hain. To kyun na hum apne bachon ke roshan mustaqbil ke liye Urdu ko tarjih dein? Yeh waqt ki zaroorat hai ke hum apni zuban ko ahmiyat dein aur apne mustaqbil ke memaron ko mazboot banayein.


3b101
Aap mujh se is baat par to mutafiq hain ke taleem hamare bachon ka haq hai aur humein unko behtareen taleemi moaqe faraham karne chahiye. Aap ki tarah, main bhi samajhti hoon ke taleem ka mayari hona nihayat zaroori hai. Is mein koi shakk nahi ke Angrezi aik ahem zubaan hai aur iska seekhna faidemand hai, lekin kya aap ne kabhi ghour kiya hai ke hum apni qaumi zubaan, Urdu ko taleem ka zariya bana ke hum apne bachon ki taleemi buniyaad ko kis tarah mazboot bana sakte hain?

University of Education Lahore ke aik haaliqa mutaale ke mutabiq, wo bachay jo apni maadari zubaan mein parhai karte hain, unki samajh aur kamyabi ka mayar zyada hota hai. Jab bachay apni zubaan mein parhte hain, to wo na sirf mawaad ko behtar tareeke se samajh sakte hain balkay azaadana tor par sochte bhi hain. Kya hum apne bachon ko ye moqa nahi dena chahte ke wo poori tarah se apne mazameen mein maharat hasil karen?

Ek aur baat, agar hum apni qaumi zubaan mein parhain, to hamari tehzeebi virsa aur qaumi shanakht ko bhi farog milta hai. Jab bachay Urdu mein parhenge, to wo na sirf apni taleem mein behtar kar kirdagi dikhayenge balkay mulki tareekh aur saqafat se bhi zyada juday rahenge. Tarbiyat yafta nafsiyat dano ka maanna hai ke bachon ki taleemi karkardagi unki zubaani maharat se judi hoti hai, aur yahi wajah hai ke duniya ke beshtar taraqqi yafta mulk apni maadari zubaan ko taleemi nizaam ka hissa banate hain.

Aap ki fikar Angrezi ki ahmiyat ke hawale se bilkul durust hai, lekin humein ye dekhna hai ke ibtidaai taleem mein kya cheez behtareen hai. Angrezi seekhne ke moaqe baad mein bhi diye ja sakte hain, lekin ibtidaai taleem ka platform acha hona chahiye.

UNICEF ki report ke mutabiq, Pakistan ke school mein bachay jo apni maadari zubaan mein taleem hasil karte hain, unki paas hone ki shirh zyada hai aur zyada bachay school khatam karte hain. Ye haqaiq humein is baat par ghour karne par majboor karte hain ke Urdu ko taleem ka zariya banana bachon ki taleemi aur zaati taraqqi ke liye behtareen ho sakta hai.

Sochiye, agar hum Urdu ko nasirf apne rozmarra ki zubaan balkay taleemi nizaam ka bhi hissa banayen, to hum kitni bari tadad mein bachon ko faida pohcha sakte hain. Mulki taraqqi ke liye zaroori hai ke hum taleem ko aasan aur qaabil-e-faham banayein, taake koi bhi bacha peeche na rahe.

Mujhe yaqeen hai ke aap bhi is baat par mutafiq honge ke hamare bachon ka behtareen mustaqbil hi hamara maqsad hona chahiye, aur isi ke liye humein unki taleem ko qaabil-e-faham aur moasar banana chahiye, jo ke hamari qaumi zubaan, Urdu mein taleem dene se mumkin hai. Kya aap bhi is rai se ittefaq nahi karte?


3b110
Dost, aap ne yeh zaroor socha hoga ke English hamari taaleemi zubaan honi chahiye kyun ke yeh aik aalaami zubaan hai, lekin aaye haqeeqat ko behtar samajhne ki koshish karte hain. Sabse pehle, tehqiqat ne dikhaya hai ke jab bachay apni madari zubaan, yani Urdu mein taleem hasil karte hain, to woh tezi se aur asar daar tareeke se seekhte hain. Kuch saal pehle, mahireen ne yeh jaiyzah liya ke sarkari schoolon ke bachay jo Urdu mein parte thay, unki taleemi kar kargai un bachon se behtar thi jinser ne English mein taleem hasil ki thi.

Hamare mulk mein bhi yahi usool laagu ho sakta hai. Yeh touknka mo'tabar idara ki report ke mutabiq "Urdu mein parhne wale bachay zyada takhleeqi aur manteqi samajh rakhte hain."

Yeh baat bhi yaad rakhen ke Shams ur Rahman Faruqi jaise Urdu zubaan ke mo'tabar musannif ne nishandahi ki hai ke "zubaan hamari saqafat aur tareekh ka hissa hai." English ko barhawa dene ka matlab hai hamari saqafat aur khuddari ko kamzor karna. Kahin aisa na ho ke hum apni shanakht kho baithein.

Aaiye hum apni taaleemi policy ka loha manwaayein aur Urdu mein taleem ko farogh dein, taake hamari aanewali naslein mazboot aur zameen se judi rahein. Agar hum sab mil kar is tabdili ko apnaayein tou yaqeen janiye, Pakistan taleem aur taraqqi mein naya urooj paa sakta hai.


3b111
Pakistan ki taraqqi aur bhalayi ke liye humein apni zuban ko ahmiyat deni hogi. Aayiye dekhte hain ke Urdu ko bator dars o tadrees ke zariya apnanay ke kya faiday hain.

Ek tehqeeq ke mutabiq, jab bachay apni madri zuban mein seekhte hain to unki taleemi karkardagi behtareen hoti hai. Yahan tak ke UNESCO ne bhi mutaddid bar yeh bataya hai ke madri zuban mein taleem hasil karne walay talaba behtar taleemi karkardagi ka muzahira karte hain.

Urdu ko apna kar hum apne taleemi nizaam ko mazid mazboot bana sakte hain. Kya aap jaante hain ke Cheen aur Japan jese mumalik ne apni zubanon mein taleem de kar kitni taraqqi ki hai? Inki tamam taleemi mawad unki qaumi zubanon mein hai, jis ki wajah se inke talaba jadeed uloom par tezi se urooj hasil kar rahe hain.

Angrezi zuban seekhna bhi zaruri hai, magar ibtidaai taleem Urdu mein dene se bachay mazboot buniyad rakhte hain. Baad mein Angrezi bhi seekhna asaan ho jata hai. Naqabile yaqeen baat yeh hai ke nabbe (90) fee-sad walidain bhi yehi chahte hain ke unke bachon ko Urdu mein taleem di jaye, kyun ke woh behtar seekh pate hain.

Pakistan mein bhi, agar Urdu ko buniyadi zariya taleem bana diya jaye to hamare talaba se behtar karkardagi dekhne ko milegi. Is tarah woh apni tareekh, saqafat aur aqdar se jude rahenge aur aalmi mayaar par poora utrein ge.

Aap soche, jab hamare bachay apni zuban ke zariye apni salahiyaton ko nikhaarein ge to woh haqeeqi ma'non mein Pakistan ka roshan mustaqbil banain ge. Taleem sirf zuban ka masla nahi, balkay ilm ko saral aur mazboot banane ka hai, aur Urdu hi is ka sabse acha zariya sabit ho sakti hai.

To aayiye, mil kar Urdu ko dars o tadrees ka buniyadi zariya banayen aur Pakistan ko taraqqi ki raah par gamzan karein.

Jab baat rozgar ki aati hai to English ki maharat kai moqe faraham karti hai jo sirf Urdu jaanne walo ko dastiab nahi aate.

Tasavur karein ke hamara bacha aala taleem ke liye kisi taraqqi yafta mulk mein jana chahta hai. Wahan wo duniya ki behtareen universities mein parh sakte hain magar English ke baghair yeh khwab adhura reh sakta hai. Kya hum apne bachon ka mustaqbil mehdood karna chahte hain, ya unhein duniya ke samne khara hone ka moqa dena chahte hain?

Yeh baat bhi yaad rakhein ke Hindustan bhi apne taleemi nizaam mein English ko bharpoor jagah de kar tezi se taraqqi kar raha hai. Hum kyun peechay rahen? Hamari zimmedari hai ke hum apne bachon ko woh tamam waseelay faraham karein jo unhein aalami meyaar ke mutabiq banayein.

Urdu ki ahmiyat apni jagah, magar English ko schools ka bunyadi zariya-e-taleem banane se hum apne bachon ko aalami dor mein shaamil kar sakte hain. Ai ye, hum apne bachon ke mustaqbil ko roshan karen. English seekhein, aage barhein, aur duniya ko bata dein ke Pakistani kisi se kam nahi.

Kyun na hum Urdu ki khoobsurti aur English ki taqat ko milayen aur apne bachon ko do zubani maahir banayen? Yeh hi hamara mustaqbil roshan karega.

Dost, main jaanti hoon ke aap yeh maante hain ke angrezi hamare skulon ke liye zaroori hai, magar zara sochiye. Hamare bani Quaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah Urdu mein taqreeren karte thay, aur Urdu hi woh zabaan hai jo poore Pakistan ko jodti hai. Urdu hamari saqafat, tareekh aur virsa hai. Kya humein apni shanakht aur saqafat ko naye nasl tak muntakil nahi karna chahiye ya kisi aur ki zabaan ko?

Angrezi seekhna zaroori hai, magar hamare bachon ki buniyadi taleem apni zabaan mein honi chahiye taake woh apni saqafat aur aqdar se jude rahen. Jab ek bacha apni zabaan mein seekhta hai, to woh zyada behtari, asaani aur khud aitmaadi se seekhta hai. Urdu hamari qaumi zabaan hai aur har shakhs isay samajhta hai, isliye yeh zabaan taleem ke liye zyada mozu hai.

Hamein angrezi ko ek zariya taleemi zabaan banane ke bajaye, Urdu ko tarjeeh deni chahiye taake hamara har bacha yaksaan moqay aur behtareen taleem hasil kar sake. Kya yeh behtar nahi hoga ke bachay apni madri zabaan mein seekhein, jis se woh zyada khud aitmad aur takhleeqi hon? Urdu hamari pehchaan hai, hamara fakhar hai, aur isay humein farog dena chahiye.

Zara sochiye, agar hum Urdu ko awwaleen zabaan banayein, to hamari naslain hamari tehzeeb o tamaddun ko samajh sakengi aur aalam mein kabhi bhi kisi se peechay nahi rahengi. Urdu mein parhai hamari community ko mazboot banayegi aur hamara ittehad barhaye gi. Kya aap nahi chahenge ke hamare bachay hamari rawayaat, kahaniyan, aur heroes ko jaanen aur samjhen?

Dost, Urdu hamari zabaan hai, hamari pehchaan hai, humein apni aane wali naslon ke mustaqbil ke liye Urdu ko apnana hoga. Sab se barh kar, humein apne bachon ko woh shanakht deni hogi jo hamare aabao ajdaad ne humein di. Urdu ko ahmiyat dena, hamare waqar ki hifazat karna hai.
